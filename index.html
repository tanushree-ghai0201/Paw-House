About Us

Around the globe, animals have been subjected to torture and mistreatment at the hands of humans far too many times but still these little souls have the fierce dedication to love humans back. We at ‘The Paw House’ are inclined to add joy to their lives. We, ‘The paw house’ are a small organization started by a young group of people who aim to provide stray dogs a loving and a caring home forever. Our aim is to spread the word amongst the maximum possible people to adopt these loving companions. We work really hard to rescue as many dogs as possible and find homes for them. We always ensure that the dog you adopt is sterilized, fully vaccinated and completely healthy. We aim to sensitize people under the slogan- Adopt Don’t Shop! Adoption means giving dogs who have been abandoned by their previous families or are living on streets, a second chance to a comfortable life. However, buying means giving encouragement to breeders who exploit dogs for money. Dog breeders exploit dog mothers for giving birth repeatedly whereas when you adopt a dog you save a life.
